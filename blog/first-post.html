5 Simple Habits for a Peaceful Mind

Published by NawalKishor • July 2025

  • Start each morning with 5 minutes of reflection.
  • Gratitude: write 3 things you’re thankful for daily.
  • Limit social media to 30 minutes/day.
  • Take a walk in nature.
  • End your day with slow, deep breaths.
