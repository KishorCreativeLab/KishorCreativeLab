Awakening to Joy: Small Practices That Brighten My Every Day

By Nawal Kishor August 6, 2025 2 min read

Welcome to KishorCreativeLab! Life is filled with ups and downs, but along the way, I’ve discovered that it’s often the smallest practices that bring the greatest joy and lightness to our days. In this post, I’d like to share how I gently invite more positivity and energy into my daily routine.

1. Welcoming the Day with a Smile

Each morning, before I even get out of bed, I make it a point to smile—no matter how I’m feeling. This simple act might sound minor, but studies show it can trick your brain into releasing feel-good chemicals. Sometimes, I pair this with a quiet moment of gratitude, thinking of one thing I’m looking forward to, even if it’s just a hot cup of tea or a pleasant walk.

2. Intentionally Seeking Moments of Play

As adults, we often forget how to play. I’ve started building little playful rituals into my day: dancing to my favorite song while making breakfast, doodling on a notepad during a break, or having a lighthearted chat with a friend. These tiny sparks of joy help me shake off stress and reconnect with my creative side.

3. Reflecting on Small Wins Before Bed

At night, I spend a minute acknowledging the day’s victories—big or small. It could be completing a task, helping someone, or simply being gentle with myself during a tough moment. Writing them down in a notebook helps me end the day on a note of self-appreciation and contentment.

Over time, these little habits have transformed my outlook, making even ordinary days brighter and more joyful. They remind me that happiness isn’t something waiting at the horizon—it’s woven into the fabric of our daily lives, ready to be noticed.

What simple practices help you tap into daily joy? Share your favorites in the comments—I’d love to celebrate them with you and learn from your experiences.
